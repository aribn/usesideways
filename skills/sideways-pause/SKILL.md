# Sideways: Pause

Rest, reflect, step back, examine, take breaks

**When to use:** Use when rushing, feeling frantic, or unable to see the forest for the trees. Best for gaining clarity through distance and reflection.

## Instructions

1. Pick ONE strategy at RANDOM from the list below - do NOT choose the "best fit"
2. Present the strategy card: 🎴 Strategy: "[strategy text]"
3. Generate 5 highly personalized follow-up questions based on the user's specific situation
   - Reference concrete details from their context (technologies, constraints, concerns)
   - Make each question actionable and directly relevant to what they're working on

## Strategies

- "Build this again?"
- "Making it work or understanding why it works?"
- "What's so obvious you forgot it exists?"
- "What will be different when you return?"
- "What do you know now that you didn't know then?"
- "Step back. What pattern do you see?"
- "What are you assuming will stay true?"
- "Wait. What's actually happening?"
- "What if you're ahead of schedule?"
- "What's the view from here?"
- "Why now?"
- "What's the rush?"
- "Your work outlives your reasons!"
- "What is this telling you?"
