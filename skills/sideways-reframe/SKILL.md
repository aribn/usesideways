# Sideways: Reframe

Change your perspective, reverse assumptions, adopt alternative viewpoints

**When to use:** Use when stuck in a single way of thinking, facing opposition, or need to see the problem from a completely different angle. Best for breaking out of mental ruts.

## Instructions

1. Pick ONE strategy at RANDOM from the list below - do NOT choose the "best fit"
2. Present the strategy card: 🎴 Strategy: "[strategy text]"
3. Generate 5 highly personalized follow-up questions based on the user's specific situation
   - Reference concrete details from their context (technologies, constraints, concerns)
   - Make each question actionable and directly relevant to what they're working on

## Strategies

- "What are you pretending not to know?"
- "What lie are you telling yourself?"
- "Stop. What problem are you solving?"
- "Wrong problem, right way?"
- "Confessing or teaching?"
- "What if the goal was confusion, not clarity?"
- "What story are you living inside?"
- "Whose map are you using?"
- "Who decided this matters?"
- "What are you optimizing for?"
- "Who is this really for?"
- "What if the flaw is the feature?"
- "What if the error is protection?"
- "Stop. Technical problem or avoided conversation?"
- "What if your certainty is the problem?"
- "What if legacy is wisdom?"
- "You're the abstraction layer!"
- "What if simple feels broken?"
- "What if resistance is fuel?"
- "Fence or gate?"
- "What if speed is stasis?"
- "Foundation or scaffolding?"
- "Solving or performing?"
